# Retold Community Code Of Conduct

This policy applies to the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, Discord, playtests, and other official Retold spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat people with respect, including during disagreement.
- Keep criticism focused on ideas, behavior, code, and outcomes.
- Give useful context when reporting problems.
- Respect privacy, authorship, licenses, and creative ownership.
- Follow reasonable moderator and developer instructions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, discrimination, or targeted abuse
- Personal attacks, repeated hostility, trolling, or disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam, scams, malicious links, impersonation, or account compromise
- Pirated material, stolen assets, or false authorship claims
- Encouraging unauthorized exploitation against players or servers

## Enforcement

Send private reports with relevant links, screenshots, or message IDs to **alex@alexejtusl.cz**. Do not retaliate against good-faith reporters.

Responses may include content removal, a private warning, temporary restrictions, removal from playtesting or contribution spaces, or a permanent ban. Severe threats, malicious activity, or illegal content may be escalated to the relevant platform or authorities.
